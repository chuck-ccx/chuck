# Industry Overview — Diamond Drilling

## 1. Big Picture: Junior Mining and Drilling

### 1.1 What a Junior Mining Company Is
- Small exploration company, no producing mine
- Lives on raised capital and "the next discovery"
- Main asset is land, data, and drill results

### 1.2 How Money Flows Into a Project
- Angel / retail / funds put money into the junior
- Private placements, flow-through shares (Canada), warrants
- Cash goes into exploration budget and G&A

### 1.3 Why Drilling Is the Big Spend
- Geophysics, mapping, boots on the ground first
- Drilling is where budgets spike and risk drops
- Market reacts mostly to drill results and grades
